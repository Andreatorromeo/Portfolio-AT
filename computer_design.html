Side project

Graphic design

The brief

For my Computer Design course at the SPD, one of the projects was to explore a specific function of Photoshop and then create a poster -for printing and digital- visually and metaphorically related to the chosen tool. Another limit was the possibility to use only one picture.

The idea

My approach was to select a picture that could be easily manipulated and then creating a whole concept basing on what that pictures meant to me

Pain points

  • The impossibility to use more than one picture
  • Creating a concept metaphorically related to the chosen tool
  • Studying and understanding the theory behind the tool

Strategy

  • Finding a meaningful picture
  • Selecting, studying the tool and relate it to the main picture
  • Creating a complex environment

The concept

I choose a picture of a starry sky and immediately I linked it to the complexity of the Universe and it's infinite shapes.

The prism

To create the illusion of an environment distorted by gravity I created a prism where the faces have tricky reflections

3D Twist

On a different file, using the 3D>Twist I created an abstract shape focusing on the deepness among the Z-axe.

Details

As last step I merged all, creating different layers of deepness and then I added some shadows and lights.
