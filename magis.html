Magis

Customization tool, UX/UI

The company

Magis was founded in 1976 in Northern Italy. For more than 40 years Magis have been creating design products for people’s homes, offices and public spaces.

Brief

The goal was to improve the overall UX and the actual customization tool, putting the user at the center and allowing him to easily customize a product.

Challenges

  • Facilitate the customization process
  • Presenting a product in an interesting way
  • Putting the user as core of every process
  • Making products easy to recognize and find

Strategy

  • Creation of a new PDP
  • Creation of a new customization tool
  • Creation of a new footer and Navbar
  • Introducing the summary after the customization
  • Introducing new features

What changed

With a new description structure, the adding of the colors option, the reduction of CTA and new icons and carousel, I tried to give to this page the unique Magis style merging aestethics and usability

Designer and cards

Related products are shown with a new card structure, which let the user to easily understand the customizable products and adding an item in the favourites.
A section dedicated to designers makes the research easier for products of the same collection.

Customization tool

The customization tool interface has been semplified, it's full screen, you can save the customization for later and now this tool can fit properly all the products; in case more than 6 colors are showed, a scrollbar will appear.

Hover

The hover is crucial for the interface and the experience. When the mouse hovers over the red dots, is now possible to see which part are you going to modify and the measures.
