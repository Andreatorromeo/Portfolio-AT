3. New Product detail page

What changed

With a new description structure, the adding of the colors option, the reduction of CTA and new icons and carousel, I tried to give to this page the unique Magis style merging aestethics and usability

Minimalism

Minimalism and structurated pages should give the chance to the user to understand products details, starting the customization, knowing more about designers and get a quick link to other related products.

New cards

Related products are shown with a new card structure, which let the user to easily understand the customizable products and add an item in the favourites.

4. Customization tool

The interface

The customization tool interface has been semplified, it's full screen, you can save the customization for later on and now this tool can fit properly all the products; in case more than 6 colors are showed, a scrollbar will appear.

Hover

The hover is crucial for the interface and the experience. When the mouse hovers over the red dots, is now possible to see which part are you going to modify and the measures.

5. Summary page and footer

New features

The product will be 3d only in this final step. It's possible now to contact the assistance for the customized product directly after the finishing of the customization. The user can generate the QR code of his product and place it in his room using the camera and share the product.
