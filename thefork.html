TheFork

The Fork is an app/website that helps people find and book a table at restaurant. It provides restaurant reviews, menu, pricing, hours and provides great deals.

Brief

This project is a group project made with Franziska Bauer and Hadeel Mohammad.
The goal was to conduct a research and basing on the results, redesign the app with the goal of increasing users’ adoption and usage.

Pain points

  • Having nourishing meals can take too much time for busy people
  • There's no tool which helps to find a restaurant with a typical cuisine of the city where the user is.
  • Impossibility for the user to manage his own reservation

Strategy

  • Creation of a preorder option
  • Creation of a batch of authenticity
  • Introduction of an "edit option" for a booking after the confirmation

What changed

We introduced a new interface for the home which includes the map navigation, a new navigation bar and quick filters on top

Restaurant page

The authentic batch gives the user a hint that the restaurant carrying the batch is offering an authentic, local cuisine.
Obtaining the batch from the app requires the restaurant to undergo and pass an evaluation form a third part.

Preorder option

The user can choose between a set menu or can create his own menu. Preordering means choosing and paying the meals for registered guests and yourself when finalising the booking process.
Upon arriving to the restaurant, your meals will arrive to your table without any waiting time.

Editing your order

The option to edit your booking once you already confirmed it, gives to the user the chance to change the date of the booking as well as the number of people who will arrive to the restaurant.
