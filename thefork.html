TheFork

The Fork is an app/website that helps people find and book a table at restaurant. It provides restaurant reviews, menu, pricing, hours and provides great deals.

Brief

The goal was to conduct a research and basing on the results, redesign the app with the goal of increasing usersâ€™ adoption and usage.

Pain points

  • Having nourishing meals can take too much time for busy people
  • There's no tool which helps to find a restaurant with a typical cuisine of the city where the user is.
  • Impossibility for the user to manage his own reservation

Strategy

  • Creation of a preorder option
  • Creation of a batch of authenticity
  • Introduction of an "edit option" for a booking after the confirmation

What changed

We introduced a new interface for the home which includes the map navigation, a new navigation bar and quick filters on top

Authentic cousine

The authentic batch gives the user a hint that the restaurant carrying the batch is offering an authentic, local cuisine. Obtaining the batch from the app requires the restaurant to undergo and pass an evaluation form a third party

Customization tool

The customization tool interface has been semplified, it's full screen, you can save the customization for later on and now this tool can fit properly all the products; in case more than 6 colors are showed, a scrollbar will appear.

Hover

The hover is crucial for the interface and the experience. When the mouse hover the red dots, is now possible to see which part are you going to modify and the measures.
